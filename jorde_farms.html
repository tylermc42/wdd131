Welcome to the Acent and Aclaim Neighborhoods in the Jorde Farms Community

With over 300 homes already built and more on the way, the area offers a perfect blend of modern living and outdoor amenities. Residents enjoy access to a sparkling community pool, a scenic park, and lush grass-breeze areas that provide a refreshing escape.
